    About Us

    The Lyre Project is a choral ensemble dedicated to connecting people to the beauty and depth of sacred choral music. We strive for musical excellence in a way that is authentic to each of us, and are inspired to make the richness of choral music accessible and personal. We find immense joy and fulfillment in making beautiful music while journeying together as professionals, encouraging each other to grow in love and holiness.

    About Us

    The Lyre Project is a choral ensemble dedicated to connecting people to the beauty and depth of sacred choral music. We strive for musical excellence in a way that is authentic to each of us, and are inspired to make the richness of choral music accessible and personal. We find immense joy and fulfillment in making beautiful music while journeying together as professionals, encouraging each other to grow in love and holiness.

    OUR SINGERS

    Chris Susanto
    Tenor
    As the Artistic Director of The Lyre Project, Chris is constantly striving to bring people closer to the beauty of sacred music. Most of his days are spent thinking about music, whether it be conducting, arranging, or directing the three choirs under his leadership. In his free time, he enjoys games, puzzles, and listening to music of all genres.
    Anthony Nootebos
    Bass
    Anthony holds a BA in Music and is a triple threat of TLP; a composer, arranger, and a singer. As a member of our ensemble, he is our second bass (as well as emergency tenor) and the only one who doesn't wear glasses.
    Chiara Susanto
    Soprano
    Art is a constant for our second soprano Chiara, who is a co-artist of Amichi Studio, a freelance tattoo artist, and art educator. She holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a specialization in Visual Art, and just ran a full marathon in Japan!
    Christine Uy
    Soprano

    Jason Lau

    Jason has been singing since he was five years old, and his range has only expanded since then— now he is a bass of TLP! He is trilingual, loves chocolate, and plays games like League, Genshin Impact, and Honkai Star Rail.
